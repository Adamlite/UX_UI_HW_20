Project Duration

Date Start - March 23 - April 6, 2023

Duration - 3 Weeks

Problem Statement

Immigrant youths and parents in Canada struggle to find and trust the educational and social support programs on the Immigrant Youth Centre website. We need to improve the communication of program information, build organization credibility, and increase enrolment.

Objectives & Goals

To increase program enrolment and user return, and for users to have more trust in the organization.

Research

Kevin, Program Manager

“We get calls from users inquiring about information that is on the website so we know they are not successfully getting to the details they need”

Yifei, Youth Worker

“Often times, users need to view the content in their native language and shy away from registration because of that blocker.”

Aside from interviewing the staff, we conducted 25 online surveys and 5 in-person interviews

Through interviewing both immigrant youths and their parents on their pain points with the current website, they said that it was: confusing, unorganized, and cluttered.

User Insight

Immigrant youths and parents, having limited knowledge of the local language and customs, need to feel confident that the programs and resources they seek are credible and beneficial to their success in adapting to Canadian life. They need reassurance that these services will be effective in helping them with their specific needs and that they will be supported throughout the process.

User Needs

  • User needs the program information presented to them clearly and be able to navigate the site without confusion or roadblocks
  • User needs reassurance the programs are credible and beneficial, which is achieved through the reliability of the site
  • User needs reminders for upcoming classes and events. Often a user will miss programs because they were not properly informed

With the data collect, we brainstormed ides and after some iterations we creates a new Task & User Flow, and a Site Map

Low-Fidelity Wireframes

Features & Functionalities

A more logical and user-friendly web flow

A filter system for users to easily navigate programs catered to their needs and situations

The ability to add programs to the user’s calendars, as well optional reminders via email/text/follow-up phone call

Final Thoughts & Future Improvements

  1. Include more languages to have the content available to more users in their native language.
  2. More filter options to refine parameters users use to search. Being able to filter:
    1. Different programs/categories
    2. Instructors
    3. Newest/Oldest
  3. Ratings and Reviews Feature.
  4. Implemented social media engagement platform into the website to keep users more connected.
  5. Add more video content.
